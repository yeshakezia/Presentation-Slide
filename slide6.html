Slide 6: 打工經驗

很久很久以前我在餐廳打工，但我覺得工作有一點累，所以後來我就離職。到現在我比較少在外面打工，只有在學校工作。但每年暑假和寒假，我通常會去工廠打工。去年暑假，我在欣興電子工作，在品管部門上班。在那邊工作不會太忙，所以我寒假的時候也是來一樣的工廠上班。可是寒假的時候，人力資源部安排我在辦公室上班。在這理工作比較好象行政人員。他的工作内容是做資料，接電話，翻譯資料，還有整理資料。這個工作也是很輕鬆但是也有一些挑戰。我需要每天看中文字，而且同時講話的時候也很快，也會用比較難的詞將工作内容，所以我有時候會聼不太懂。不過，只些經驗讓我學到很多，也讓我瞭解辦公室工作是怎麽樣的。

A long, long time ago, I worked part-time at a restaurant, but I felt the work was a bit tiring, so I eventually resigned. Since then, I rarely work part-time outside and only work at school. However, every summer and winter break, I usually go to work at a factory. Last summer, I worked at Unimicron (Xinxing Electronics) in the Quality Control (QC) department. Working there wasn't too busy, so I went back to work at the same factory during the winter break. However, during the winter break, the Human Resources department arranged for me to work in the office. Working here is more like being an administrative staff member. The job content includes data entry, answering phones, translating documents, and organizing files. This job is also quite relaxed, but it has some challenges. I need to look at Chinese characters every day， meanwhile, people speak very quickly and use more difficult vocabulary to discuss work, so sometimes I don't quite understand everything. Nonetheless, these experiences have taught me a lot and let me understand what office work is really like.
