Slide 4: 學習過程

因爲我是轉系學生，所以我的學習過程跟別的學生不太一樣。去年的時候，我是大一，但是我選了很多大二的課程，因爲大一的課已經滿了。所以大二的時候我上了一些大一的課程。雖然一開始有點不習慣，但我慢慢調整，也學會了怎麽安排自己的學習進度。

Because I am a transfer student, my learning process is a bit different from other students. Last year, when I was a freshman, but I took many sophomore (Year 2) courses because the freshman courses were already full. Therefore, during my sophomore year, I took some freshman (Year 1) courses. Although I wasn't used to it at first, but I slowly adjusted， also learned how to arrange my own study progress.
