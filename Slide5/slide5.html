Slide 5: 去年的日常生活

我去年的大學生活就好像別的大學生一樣，沒有什麽特別的。平日去上課還有在學校上班（有時候我也會去做運動或是參加一些活動），假日還是有空時就跟朋友去玩，暑假和寒假時就去打工。

我去年日常生活中的活動：（上課，上班，出去玩，打麻將，打牌，打撞球，騎脚踏車，打羽毛球，彈吉他，跑步，彈鋼琴）

My university life last year was just like any other student's—nothing really special. On weekdays, I went to classes and worked at the school (sometimes I’d also exercise or join some activities). On weekends or whenever I had free time, I’d hang out with friends. During summer and winter breaks, I worked part-time.

My daily activities last year: (Attending class, going to work, going out/hang out, playing mahjong, playing cards, playing pool, riding a bicycle, playing badminton, playing guitar, running, playing piano.)
